Rural Access Programme (RAP)

Sannighat – Shipkhana Road (19Km) and Ramnakot Road (5 km) in Kalikot district.

Dingla and Bhojpur – Ghodetar district Roads in Bhojpur district

Diktel – Khotang Road in Khotang district (50 Km)

Rural Access Programme

Design Review and Construction supervision

Services

Detail Survey & design modification of various sections.

Preparation of detail cost for completion of road. Carried out alignment setting and construction survey works including verification of cadastral survey. Preparation of progress reports. Conduct technical training, Construction supervision of both roads adopting labor based participatory and environment friendly approach.

Slope stabilization and application of bio-engineering measures for erosion control. Organized approach orientation and road supervisor & RBG training. Support to NGO in RBG/SBG selection, social mobilization, peoples’ participation and public audits Coordination with the local institutions like VDC, DDC, DRCC, UC/UG, Local NGOs, line agencies. Establishment and monitoring of road maintenance system. Preparation of as-built drawings.
