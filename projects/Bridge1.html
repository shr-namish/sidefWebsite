Project information

  • Category: Road, Bridge & Transport Infrastructure
  • Client: Government of Nepal, Department of Road, Bridge Branch
  • Project Start Date: February, 2015
  • Project End Date: July, 2015
  • Scope of Services: Siraha, Dhanusa, Lalitpur

Project Description

The Department of Road, Bridge Branch utilized the services of engineering consulting firms in the fields of soil investigation, hydrological studies, bridge engineering, river training works, environmental assessment etc. for providing engineering consulting services for detailed engineering design work of proposed 7 RCC T Beam Bridges and 1 pre stretched Belauti River Bridge(40m) including river training works, and approach roads. The objective of this job was to design a safe, reliable and cost-effective bridge using the appropriate technology

Services

Detailed survey, soil investigation, hydrological study, environmental assessment, detailed engineering design and cost estimate of the motorable bridges as detailed below
