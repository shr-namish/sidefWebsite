Project information

  • Category: Water Supply & Sanitaion
  • Client: Kathmandu Valley Water Supply Management Board
  • Project Start Date: November, 2022
  • Project End Date: October, 2024
  • Scope of Services: Bagmati Province, Nepal

Project Description

The main task of the consulting service is to conduct the Detail Engineering Design of all the components of the Sisneri Water Supply System including Detail Drawing, Cost Estimate and preparation of Procurement Documents Works. The consultant also shall prepare the scoping document and Terms of Reference (ToR) for the Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA) of Sisneri Water Supply Project and (ii) Preparation of Detailed TOR for Design of the Sisneri Dam. The Consultant shall follow National/International best practice during survey, design, and report preparation in close coordination with KVWSMB. Total project Cost: NPR 33,077,714,105

Services

Type of Scheme

  • Dam storage with multi stage pumping system

  • Submersible Pumps: 21 sets installed at the dam with a capacity of 110 LPS each.

    • Total Pipe Length: 27.03 km
    • Pipe Material: Ductile Iron
    • Valve and Accessories: SCADA friendly; rated pressure ≥2 times working pressure

    • Length: 8690m
    • Pipe Diameter and Nos: 3 Nos of 800mm

    Water Treatment Plant

    Type

  • Conventional (Flocculation, Sedimentation, Rapid Sand Filter, Chlorination)

    • Capacity

  • 140 MLD (1.62 m³/s)

    • Components

  • Receiving Chamber: RCC rectangular tank, detention time of 4.19 minutes.
  • Distribution Chamber: Weir Type, 298.72 m³, detention time 3.07 minutes.
  • Coagulant Mixing: 7 Nos, weir type using potential energy.
  • Flocculation: Vertical-baffling type, 7 Nos, detention time 30 minutes.
  • Sedimentation: Horizontal flow type, 7 Nos, detention time 128 minutes.
  • Sludge Discharge: Hopper type, 84 Nos, capacity 16.9 m³ each.
  • Rapid Sand Filtration: 28 chambers, constant flow with surface washing.
  • Clear Water Storage: 2 Nos, each 3150 m³, detention time 65 minutes.
  • Chemical Dosing: Six chemical mixing tanks and three dosing tanks.
  • Sterilizing Agent: Bleaching powder.
  • Filter Washing: Backwashing by filtered water, backwash rate 0.6 m³/m²/min.
