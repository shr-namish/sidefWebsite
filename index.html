About Us

Welcome to SIDeF

Established in 1994, Sustainable infrastructure development foundation(SIDeF) is a consultancy and research organisation in the field of sustainable rural infrastructure development with community participation. It has provided its services at present and in past in more than 30 districts of Nepal. SIDeF’s major strength lies in the planning and construction of low cost, environment friendly rural road construction. The organisation has gained extensive experience in working with the local government and the local community through partnership approach resulting in sustainable project output.

Leadership

Our Team

Govind N. Mallik

Director, Management, Environmentalist, Operation

Mr. Govind Narayan Mallik is a dedicated environmentalist. Holding an M.Phil. in Environmental Science, he has over 35 years of experience working on projects related to sustainable infrastructure development. He was also a team member of the Dhading Development Project (DDP/GTZ), where the innovative “Green Road Approach” was initiated.

Hare Ram Shrestha

Director, Technical and Transportation

Dr. Hare Ram Shrestha is an infrastructure specialist. He holds a Ph.D. in Developing Sustainability Criteria for Urban Transportation and has over 35 years of experience in sustainable infrastructure development. Dr. Shrestha has held prominent positions, including President of the Nepal Engineers Association (NEA) and President of the Society of Consulting Architectural and Engineering Firms (SCAEF), Nepal. He was also a team member of the Dhading Development Project (DDP/GTZ), where the innovative “Green Road Approach” was initiated

Umesh K. Shrestha

Director, Administration and Business

Mr. Umesh Kumar Shrestha is a Social Development Specialist with a master’s degree in Sociology/Anthropology and over 28 years of experience in social safeguard initiatives. He specializes in social and community development, gender, land acquisition, resettlement, and Indigenous Peoples/Ethnic Minority planning, along with monitoring and evaluation. He has contributed to major irrigation, transport, water supply, and sanitation projects of the Government of Nepal, as well as INGO programs funded by the EU, ADB, and the World Bank.

Dhruba Raj Thapa

Director, Business Development

Mr. Thapa is a seasoned Civil and Construction Engineer with over 30 years of experience in construction, consulting, and project management. He holds a B.E. in Civil Engineering, an M.Sc. in Construction Management, and a P.G. Diploma in Construction Management. He is actively involved in professional and social organizations, including serving as President of the Nepal Engineers Association (NEA) and the Cement Manufacturers’ Association.
